Non-Stick Surface

A smooth surface that lets dough glide effortlessly — no extra flour needed. Roll thin or thick, it moves with you. Flexible, durable & long-lasting.

BPA free. Teflon free. Non-toxic & odorless. Heat resistant up to 230°C. Safe for everyday cooking and direct food contact — at home or in professional kitchens.
